In Day 5 we come across a website called Reeborg's World, in which we have to guide our Robot(Reeborg) to the goal 
using our knowledge about what we have learned so far.

Reeborg's world is intended to help beginners to learn programming, using Python (Javascript is also supported).
You can head over to this link to get going - https://reeborg.ca/reeborg.html

We hepled Reeborg to win the Hurdle race by guiding him path to goal using game's built-in functions and our logic
to win. Simple yet Tricky game!
